Projects

Eden


Awarded best research, best maker and first overall prize in University of Toronto's Sustainability Makerspace Hackathon. Eden is an Android application that calculates the amount of carbon an individual produces from everyday consumption of food. It also tracks the users' carbon footprint and records it in a database displaying their eating trends and carbon emissions. The aim of this app is to bring awareness to people's meat consumption and how different foods affect the planet in different ways.
